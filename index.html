About me…

I'm an elementary particle physicist and PhD student at the University of Hamburg, working on the CMS experiment at CERN's Large Hadron Collider (LHC). My current interest is the precise determination of the mass of the top quark, the heaviest known elementary particle in the standard model of particle physics.

I studied physics at the RWTH Aachen University, where I was involved in the search for supersymmetry with the CMS experiment, mainly targeting electroweak production of supersymmetric particles decaying to photons and gravitinos.
