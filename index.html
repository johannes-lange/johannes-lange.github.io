About me…

I'm an elementary particle physicist and PhD student at the University of Hamburg, working on the CMS experiment at CERN's Large Hadron Collider (LHC). My current interest is the measurement of the mass of the top quark, the heaviest known elementary particle.

I studied physics at the RWTH Aachen University, where I was involved in the search for supersymmetry with the CMS experiment at the I. Institute of Physics B.
